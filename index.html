Features

Everything You Need to Succeed

VitaPlate combines intelligent meal planning with comprehensive tracking to make healthy eating effortless.

🍽️

Smart Meal Plans

Get personalized 7-day meal plans tailored to your calorie goals, dietary restrictions, and food preferences. Automatic adjustments based on your progress.

🔄

Easy Meal Swaps

Don't like a meal suggestion? Swap it instantly with smart alternatives that match your nutritional targets. Each option shows a compatibility score.

📊

Macro Tracking

Track your protein, carbs, fats, and fiber with beautiful visual progress bars. Stay on target with real-time updates throughout the day.

🥗

Recipe Database

Access hundreds of nutritious recipes with full ingredient lists, cooking instructions, and detailed macro breakdowns per serving.

💪

Workout Logging

Log your workouts and see how exercise impacts your daily calorie budget. Track cardio, strength training, and more.

⚙️

Dietary Restrictions

Set your allergies and dietary restrictions once. VitaPlate automatically filters out recipes containing gluten, dairy, nuts, and more.

Getting Started

How VitaPlate Works

Start your health journey in three simple steps.

1

Set Your Goals

Enter your profile details, choose your fitness goal (cut, maintain, or bulk), and set any dietary restrictions.

2

Get Your Plan

VitaPlate generates a personalized 7-day meal plan with balanced nutrition tailored to your exact calorie needs.

3

Track & Achieve

Log your meals and workouts, swap meals you don't like, and watch your progress as you crush your goals.
