Meal Planning

Your Week,
Planned for You

Your personalized 7-day meal plan is generated based on your exact calorie needs, fitness goals, and dietary preferences. Each day includes breakfast, lunch, dinner, and snacks — all balanced to hit your macro targets without you doing any of the math.

Don't like a suggestion? Tap any meal to instantly see smart swap alternatives ranked by compatibility score, so you always know how well each option fits your plan before you commit. Swap as many times as you want.

VitaPlate also learns from your previous week's food log. If you consistently hit your targets, the next plan reinforces what's working. If you miss meals or go over calories, the recommendations adjust accordingly.

Macro Calculator

Know Exactly What You're Cooking

Paste any recipe's ingredient list and get a full macro breakdown — per ingredient and per serving — in seconds.

Step 1 · Input

Paste Any
Ingredient List

Found a recipe online or in a cookbook? Copy the ingredient list, paste it straight into the Macro Calculator, set how many servings the recipe makes, and tap Parse & Calculate. VitaPlate reads every single line — identifying the ingredient name, quantity, and unit automatically.

It handles cups, tablespoons, teaspoons, ounces, grams, and natural language quantities like "handful", "2 strips", or "1 cup (5 oz)". No formatting required — just paste what the recipe says and let the calculator do the work.

Prefer to build a recipe from scratch? You can also add ingredients one by one using the manual entry fields with a dropdown unit selector. Mix and match paste and manual entries in the same calculation.

📋 Paste Any Recipe ⚡ Auto-Parse ✏️ Manual Entry Option
Step 2 · Results

Full Breakdown,
Per Serving

Once calculated, every ingredient is listed with its matched name, total weight in grams, and individual color-coded macro chips — orange for calories, blue for protein, orange-amber for carbs, red for fat, and green for fiber. Every number is per the quantity you specified.

Approximate matches are flagged with a warning icon so you can tap directly on the ingredient name to correct it if the automatic match wasn't quite right. Confidence in your numbers matters.

Scroll to the bottom for the full recipe totals and a clean per-serving breakdown divided by however many servings you set. All data sourced from USDA FoodData Central — the same database used by nutrition professionals and researchers.
