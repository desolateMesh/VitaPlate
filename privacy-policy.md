# Privacy Policy for VitaPlate

**Last Updated: January 25, 2026**

## Overview

VitaPlate ("the App") is a nutrition and fitness tracking application developed by Jason Rochau. Your privacy is important to me.

**The short version: All your data stays on your device. I don't collect, transmit, or share any of your personal information.**

## Information Collection and Use

### Data Stored Locally on Your Device

VitaPlate stores the following information **locally on your device only**:

- **Profile Information**: Height, weight, age, sex, activity level, and fitness goals
- **Dietary Preferences**: Allergies and dietary restrictions you configure
- **Meal Logs**: Foods you log, including calories and macronutrient data
- **Meal Ratings**: Your ratings of recipes (1-5 stars)
- **Workout Data**: Exercise sessions you record
- **Meal Plans**: Generated meal recommendations

This data is stored using Android's local storage (Room Database and DataStore) and **never leaves your device**.

### Data I Do NOT Collect

VitaPlate does **NOT** collect, transmit, or have access to:

- Your name, email address, or contact information
- Your location
- Device identifiers or advertising IDs
- Usage analytics or crash reports
- Any data transmitted to external servers

## Data Sharing

I do not share any of your data with third parties because I do not have access to it. Your data exists only on your device.

## Data Security

Your data is stored locally on your device using Android's secure storage mechanisms. The security of your data depends on your device's security settings (screen lock, encryption, etc.).

## Data Retention and Deletion

Your data remains on your device until you:
- Manually delete it within the App
- Uninstall the App (which removes all App data)
- Clear the App's data through Android settings

## Children's Privacy

VitaPlate is not directed at children under the age of 13. I do not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. The App is intended for users who are managing their own nutrition and fitness goals.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

I may update this Privacy Policy if future app updates add features that change how your data is handled, such as cloud backup or analytics. Any such features would require downloading a new version from the Play Store. Changes will be reflected by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this document.
