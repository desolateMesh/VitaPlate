Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 27, 2026

VitaPlate ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

VitaPlate is designed with privacy in mind. All your data is stored locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on external servers.

The information you enter into VitaPlate includes:

  • Profile information (age, weight, height, activity level)
  • Fitness goals and dietary restrictions
  • Meal logs and food entries
  • Workout logs
  • Recipe ratings and preferences

How Your Data Is Stored

All data is stored locally on your device using Android's secure storage mechanisms. Your information never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any servers.

Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, rent, or trade any of your personal information with third parties. Since all data remains on your device, there is no data to share.

Third-Party Services

VitaPlate does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. We do not use cookies or similar tracking technologies.

Data Security

Your data is protected by your device's built-in security features. We recommend using a secure lock screen on your device to protect your information.

Data Deletion

You can delete all your data at any time by:

  • Clearing the app's data through your device's Settings
  • Uninstalling the VitaPlate app

Children's Privacy

VitaPlate is intended for users 18 years of age and older. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 18.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
